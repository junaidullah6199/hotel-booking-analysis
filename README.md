# Hotel Booking Cancellation Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes hotel booking data to understand cancellation behavior and identify business strategies to reduce revenue loss.

## Business Problem
City and Resort hotels are experiencing high cancellation rates, affecting occupancy and profitability.

## Tools & Technologies
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

## Dataset
- 2015â€“2017 hotel booking data
- Includes reservation status, average daily rate (ADR), country, customer type, and booking channel

## Key Insights
- ~37% of bookings were canceled.
- Higher ADR (Average Daily Rate) leads to higher cancellation.
- January shows highest cancellation rate.
- Majority of bookings come from Online Travel Agencies.
- Portugal has the highest cancellation volume.

## Business Recommendations
- Adjust pricing strategy during high-cancellation periods.
- Offer targeted discounts during peak cancellation months.
- Improve service quality in high-risk regions.
- Reduce reliance on high-cancellation booking channels.
